About Me

Hello! My name is Geoff Davis. I am a senior web design & interactive media student. Upon my impending graduation in May of 2015, I will pursue employment opportunities pertaining to creating interactive and unique web spaces, including but not limited to web design, user experience, user interface, and development of interactive/responsive sites.

Education

From grades 9 through 12, I attended Central High School in Crystal Lake, Illinois. It was here that I first found interest in digital design, after taking a course in computer graphics; however, due to the lack of any web design courses, I focused many of my elective hours on social science and history.

For my secondary education, I am attending Harding University in Searcy, Arkansas. As stated above, I am working towards a Bachelor of Arts in the field of Web Design & Interactive Media, through the Department of Communication.

Other than my degree, I have been involved in the social club process, acting as a Charter Member, Historian, and Vice President of Omega Phi as well as a Beau (honored male member of a girl's social club) for Delta Nu. I have also been involved in intramural sports, international programs, Spring Sing, and, starting this year, the Campus Activities Board.

Background

I was born and raised in the Chicago suburb of Crystal Lake, IL. Since I was little, I enjoyed creating things and using my (some would say...overactive) imagination to have fun. I loved creating scenes and structures with LEGOs, playing with my large collection of Hot Wheels cars, and drawing.

Apart from using my imagination and creativity, I enjoyed playing sports. Early on, I started playing tee-ball and soccer; I only continued with the latter, however. I have played soccer for as long as I can remember, and I met many of my childhood friends from the many soccer teams that I played on.
